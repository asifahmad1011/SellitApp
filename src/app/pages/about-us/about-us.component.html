Sellit is a online marketplace, developed by the students of Hochschule Fulda - Global Software Development 2019

The Sellit marketplace is a platform for buying and selling services and goods such as electronics, fashion items, furniture, household goods, cars and bikes.

Sellit online marketplace is a type of e-commerce site where product or service information is provided by multiple students, whereas listing is provided by us and transactions for selling is handled by students: who list their products. Online marketplaces are the primary type of multichannel ecommerce and can be a way to expand reach.
